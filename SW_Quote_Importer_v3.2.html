Quote Importer

Extract supplier quotation data directly into your spreadsheet — no typing required.

How to use
1
Choose supplier
Click the tab at the top for whichever supplier sent the quote.
2
Upload & extract
Drop the PDF onto the upload area, then click Extract Data.
3
Copy & paste
Click Copy All Rows, go to your spreadsheet, click the first empty cell in column A, and press Ctrl+V.
Before pasting — check the Calculated Total matches the PDF total. If they match it turns green, meaning all line items were found correctly. Multi-page quotes are handled automatically. You can extract a different PDF without refreshing the page.
